About us

Smiling Skin is established in 2019, and is a website in Australia to work on skin cancer in Victoria, from research to prevention and support. The website mainly help international students aged 19 to 24, give them medical advice and protection recommendation, and provide the function of locating the appropriate skin clinic.

Our vision

The vision of Smiling Skin is to raise awareness of skin cancer among international university students aged from 19 to 24 and improve the wellbeing of people who suffer from cancer, provide treatment advice to potential patients, offer protection advice to the general public.
