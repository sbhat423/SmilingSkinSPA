OUR GOAL

We believed that the international student community should be aware of the dangers of skin cancer. Some could have just flowed in from another country or maybe planning a trip to "hotspots" in Australia. Most of them don't know how damaging the UV rays can be in Australia, especially during the Summer seasons.

Therefore, we want to prepare them to defend themselves against the Sun by providing the necessary tools to avoid getting skin cancer. That's why, we are committed to helping raise awareness of skin cancer among them.
