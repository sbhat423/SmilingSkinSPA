MELANOMA SKIN CANCER IN AUSTRALIA Published: 28/03/2019 From Australian Government

In 2016, there were 1,281 deaths from melanoma skin cancer in Australia (863 males and 418 females). In 2019, it is estimated that this will increase to 1,725deaths (1,190 males and 536 females). In 2019, it is estimated that the risk of an individual dying from melanoma skin cancer by their 85th birthday will be 1 in 134 (1 in 89 males and 1 in 246 females).
