What is Skin Cancer?

Skin cancer occurs in epidermal cells, the top or outer layer of the skin; Ultraviolet (UV) radiation penetrates the skin and damages the DNA of skin cells; If the body is unable to repair the damage, cells begin to divide and grow uncontrollably; This growth eventually leads to tumours (Cancer Council Australia, 2019).
