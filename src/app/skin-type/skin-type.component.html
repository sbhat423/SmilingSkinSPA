WHAT ARE THE DIFFERENT SKIN TYPES?

Did you know that there are different skin types and each of them has a different reaction to the sun. You can't tell what skin type you are by just looking at your own skin. Therefore, we'll be using a skin typing technique developed by Fitzpatrick called "Fitzpatrick skin typing". In short, this guy scientifically classify skins into different types based on the skin reactions to the sun.

Below is a simple quiz to determine your skin type. Just answer these simple questions and you will know your skin type in a matter of seconds. Once you get to know your skin type from the quiz, you can take the right measures to protect yourself and your skin.
